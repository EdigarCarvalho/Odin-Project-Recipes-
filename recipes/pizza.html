Catupiry and Chicken Pizza

Go back to homepage

How to make this delicious pizza? :))

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pizza crust (or you can make your own)
  • 1 cup shredded cooked chicken breast
  • 1 cup Catupiry cheese (or cream cheese as a substitute)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional, for garnish)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven as per the pizza crust package instructions.
  2. Roll out the pizza crust on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  3. Spread tomato sauce on the crust, leaving a border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle shredded cooked chicken breast over the sauce.
  5. Place dollops of Catupiry cheese or cream cheese on top of the chicken.
  6. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the entire pizza.
  7. Season with salt and pepper.
  8. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven until cheese melts and crust turns golden brown.
  9. Allow the pizza to cool for a few minutes.
  10. Optionally, garnish with fresh basil leaves.
  11. Slice and serve your delicious Catupiry and Chicken Pizza.
