Eating a tangerine is simple and refreshing. Here's a basic guide to enjoy a tangerine:

Ingredients:

  • Fresh tangerine
  • No dog needed

Instructions:

  1. Select a ripe tangerine with bright orange skin and a sweet aroma.
  2. Wash the tangerine and pat it dry.
  3. Remove the stem (optional).
  4. Pierce the top center of the tangerine and peel the skin away.
  5. Separate the tangerine into segments.
  6. Enjoy the segments, being mindful of any seeds.
  7. Continue peeling and enjoying the segments.
  8. Savor the juicy and refreshing taste of the tangerine.
